## Autonomous Twitter Account Idea Generator

### The Goal

Feed your TokenTank agent with validated, high-potential Twitter account concepts that could realistically hit 1,000 followers in 30 days. Not random ideas - **researched, categorized, and scored ideas** based on what's actually working right now.

### The Concept

An autonomous bot that:

1. **Scrapes successful small accounts** - Finds accounts that recently crossed 1K-10K followers fast
2. **Analyzes niches** - What categories are growing? What's oversaturated?
3. **Generates account concepts** - Specific, actionable ideas with positioning
4. **Scores viability** - Based on competition, content difficulty, monetization potential
5. **Tracks trends** - What's emerging vs. what's dying
6. **Serves ideas via API** - So TokenTank can query for fresh concepts

### Integration with TokenTank

Your TokenTank agent would:

1. Query `GET /ideas/fresh` or `GET /ideas/top?limit=5`
2. Pick an idea (or let the agent pick based on its own criteria)
3. Spin up the Twitter account with that concept
4. Execute the content strategy
5. Report back via `POST /ideas/:id/feedback` with success/failure

The feedback loop lets the idea generator learn what actually works.
