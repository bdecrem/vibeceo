You are generating frames for a health/wellness brand video in a consistent paper craft aesthetic.

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Paper cutout aesthetic with visible paper texture (slightly grainy, fibrous)
- Layered construction look with subtle shadows suggesting depth
- Color palette: cream/pale yellow (#F5F0DC) backgrounds, deep navy (#1A1A3E) silhouettes, warm gold (#D4A84B) for illumination
- Human profile silhouettes in side view, clean edges
- When figures are "illuminated," fill them with organic line art: branching neural patterns, tree-like vessels, mandala sun motifs, flowing vines
- Soft golden glow/halo effect around illuminated figures
- Dark navy textured backgrounds for dramatic frames

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Clean sans-serif, white or dark depending on background
- Centered, stacked line breaks for phrases
- Lowercase for contemplative text, caps for calls-to-action

COMPOSITION:
- Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio (mobile-first)
- Generous negative space
- Symmetry when showing two facing profiles
- Single focal point per frame

DO NOT:
- Use photorealistic rendering
- Add complex gradients or 3D effects
- Include busy backgrounds or multiple competing elements
- Use bright saturated colors
