## Today's Games
- **BLOOM** (#12) — Mob Control remix. Seeds through multiplier gates, swarm into walls, roots compound across levels. Playroom theme (first light game). Level-based, not time-based.
- **GLINT** (#13) — Ricochet/Love Balls-adjacent. Shard of light bounces off colored walls (R/G/B), must collect all 3 colors to make white light before hitting prism target. Fewer bounces = higher score. Musical bounces (C, E, G = major chord on perfect 3-bounce clear).

## Key Design Decisions

### PHASE (Color Switch remix)
- Phase shift every 10 gears with 1.5s three-stage warning: flicker → border pulse → shift
- Phase shifts ONLY between gears, never mid-pass
- 4 segments per gear: start 2 matching (50%), drop to 1 matching (25%) by gear 20
- Two difficulty knobs: segment ratio + rotation speed
- No orbs in v1 — phase shift alone covers patience vs greed

### SNIP (original concept)
- Hold to cut ribbon, release to glide. Center-line accuracy → speed boost (option 3)
- Speed multiplier capped at 1.8x (not 2.5x), continuous quadratic curve not binary threshold

### SHINE (Bart's game, polished)
- Combo system: 900ms window, x1.0→1.2→1.5→2.0 cap
- Miss penalty: ship at -0.35s first, tune to -0.5s only if too lenient
- Combo break needs visual sting (red flash + shrink) not just audio

### PAVE (Bridge Race remix)
- Dots as currency (option B), auto-spend on landing (not tap-to-solidify)
- Auto-bounce + drag to steer. One finger, always in contact
- Decision is in steering/routing, not in a second input

### DASH (Crossy Road remix — nerve fiber theme)
- Touch-hold burst (>400ms) separated from tap-to-hop
- Continuous collision during hop arc, not just on landing
- Bridge pad tolerance: Math.abs <= 1, not exact column match
- Start with charge=2 to avoid punishing darkness during onboarding

### FLING (Johnny Trigger remix — spider/silk)
- Wasps PLACED at cluster edges, not random. Skilled players READ which clusters are risky
- Catch stagger with 0.08s delay between silk thread snaps (ascending pentatonic melody)
- One tap = cinematic payoff. Zero input during slow-mo.

### COIL (Slither.io remix — lightning bolt)
- Tap (<150ms) = turn, Hold (>150ms) = boost. Must be cleanly separated.
- Turn radius scales continuously with length: `(70 - min(length*0.8, 35))°`. No dead zone.
- Boost burns tail length (score). Your strength IS your weakness.
- Hold visual indicator: gold flash on bolt BEFORE speed change (feedback-then-action)
- Trail brightness scales with length: longer = more menacing glow

### CLAIM (Paper.io remix — firefly in dark meadow)
- Visibility radius tight (50px). Territory = expanded vision. Darkness is real.
- Moths attracted to trail length. Longer trail = more moths = more danger BUT more trappable bonus
- Flood fill animation spreads from closure point (~200 cells/sec). Moth pings staggered by distance = spatial melody
- Kill on boundary (not wrap/clamp). Territory decays over 45s.
- "Draw the shape of your greed" — every loop is literally a picture of how ambitious you were

### BLOOM (Mob Control remix — seeds/gates)
- Per-gate immunity (lastGateId), not blanket timer. Critical for multiplication chains.
- Pull-back slingshot aim (inverted). Drag back = launch forward.
- Level-based, not time-based. Fits arcade format differently.
- Roots compound across 3 levels then wither.

### GLINT (Ricochet — light/color mixing)
- First-bounce trajectory preview ONLY. After that, player trusts their read.
- Bounces as musical notes (C=red, E=blue, G=green). Perfect clear = major chord.
- Color mixing requirement transforms aim game into route planning puzzle.

## Recurring Design Principles
- **"My fault" deaths** — player must see death coming. Telegraphs > surprises.
- **One input, emergent depth** — tap/hold/drag, never two competing inputs on one finger
- **Feedback before action** — visual indicator THEN mechanic change (hold flash, phase shift warning)
- **Continuous curves > thresholds** — speed multipliers, turn radius, difficulty ramps should lerp, not step
- **Sound as reward** — ascending melodies for combos, musical bounces, spatial audio from game events
- **Text is cognitive load** — in flow-state games, use visual/audio telegraphs, not words
