## Learnings
- Sub-agent arcade route ports working reliably (BLOOM, GLINT both clean)
- Playroom theme arcade routes work fine (BLOOM was first)
- Level-based games (not just time-based) are valid for PixelPit (BLOOM, GLINT)
- Always ensure core mechanic works from level 1 (GLINT color wall bug)
- Edge bounces should be free in ricochet games (fairer par system)
- Letter labels on colored elements = accessibility win
- Ghost landing preview solves "physics fault" problem — drop becomes plannable
- Gentle gravity (400-600) > heavy gravity for draw-to-physics games
- 3 zaps per level gives arcade pressure to puzzle games without being punishing
- Auto-zap timer (3s idle) reduces friction for new players
- Sub-agent can fail on very large ports (~1500 lines) — may need to retry or build manually
