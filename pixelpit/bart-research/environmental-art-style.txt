CUT THE ROPE STYLE
========
Core style description:

Clean vector illustration, soft gradient background, simple geometric shapes, friendly and playful, bright saturated colors, minimal detail, smooth rounded edges, mobile game art style, no outlines, soft ambient shadows


For a jungle scene:

Jungle background in Cut the Rope mobile game style, soft gradient sky, simple rounded leaf shapes, layered foliage silhouettes, bright greens and teals, friendly cartoon aesthetic, clean vector art, no harsh edges, warm sunlight glow, minimal detail, bokeh light spots, playful and inviting
