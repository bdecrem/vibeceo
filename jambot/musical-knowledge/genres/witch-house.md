# Witch House
*Dark Electronic • Horror Aesthetic • Occult Sampling*

**BPM Range:** 60-100 (heavily slowed)  
**Key Period:** 2009-2011 (peak), 2019+ (revival)  
**Geographic Origin:** New Orleans, New York City  
**Also Known As:** Drag, Screwgaze, Haunted House, Crunk Shoegaze

---

## Lineage

**Direct Ancestors:**
- **Chopped & Screwed** (DJ Screw, Houston hip-hop) - The foundational slowing and pitch-shifting techniques
- **Industrial/EBM** - Harsh textures and aggressive sound design
- **Shoegaze** - Wall-of-sound reverb processing and ethereal atmospherics
- **Dark Ambient** - Occult atmosphere and horror-inspired soundscapes
- **Gothic Rock** - Dark aesthetic and supernatural themes

**Parallel Movements:**
- **Chillwave** (2009-2011) - Emerged simultaneously as internet microgenres
- **Seapunk** - Fellow Tumblr-born aesthetic microgenre  
- **Vaporwave** - Shared nostalgic degradation and internet culture

**Cultural Context:**
Witch house emerged from the convergence of occult internet culture, Tumblr aesthetics, and the democratization of music production. Born during the transition from MySpace to Tumblr-era microgenres, it represented a rejection of mainstream accessibility through deliberate obscurity.

## Sonic DNA

### Core Characteristics
- **Heavily pitched-down vocals** - Often to inhuman registers, creating otherworldly texture
- **Stuttering trap-style drums** - Hip-hop influenced but processed through distortion
- **Massive reverb/delay processing** - Creating cavernous, supernatural spaces
- **Occult/horror samples** - Film dialogue, ritualistic sounds, supernatural elements
- **Dense bass frequencies** - Sub-bass emphasis borrowed from Houston hip-hop
- **Noise atmospherics** - Industrial textures creating unease and darkness

### Frequency Profile
- **Sub-bass dominance** (20-60Hz) - Deep, crushing low-end presence
- **Midrange scooping** (200-2kHz) - Creating hollow, ghostly character
- **High-frequency harshness** (8kHz+) - Digital distortion and noise elements
- **Extreme dynamic contrast** - Whisper-quiet passages vs. overwhelming sections

### Temporal Characteristics
- **Extremely slow tempos** (60-80 BPM typical)
- **Stuttered rhythm patterns** - Granular chopping techniques
- **Extended drone passages** - Sustained tones and ambient interludes
- **Polyrhythmic complexity** - Multiple conflicting time signatures

## Sound Design

### Vocal Processing Chain
1. **Extreme pitch shifting** (-12 to -24 semitones common)
2. **Heavy reverb** (hall/cathedral settings with 8+ second decays)
3. **Parallel compression** with harsh digital limiting
4. **Granular delay effects** creating stuttered repetitions
5. **Saturation/distortion** for digital harshness

### Drum Programming
- **Source material:** TR-808 samples, trap kits, industrial percussion
- **Processing:** Heavy compression, bit-crushing, reverse reverbs
- **Pattern style:** Syncopated trap rhythms slowed to 60-80 BPM
- **Layering:** Multiple conflicting rhythmic elements creating chaos

### Synth Architecture
- **Analog modeling** with extreme resonance and filter modulation
- **Wavetable synthesis** for evolving, otherworldly textures
- **FM synthesis** for metallic, bell-like tones
- **Granular synthesis** for textural manipulation

### Sampling Techniques
- **Source material:** Horror films, occult documentaries, vintage TV/radio
- **Processing:** Time-stretching, reverse processing, granular manipulation
- **Integration:** Samples as rhythmic elements, not just atmospheric additions
- **Layering:** Multiple samples creating narrative collages

## Production & Mixing

### Session Architecture
```
STEMS ORGANIZATION:
├── Drums (processed trap patterns)
├── Sub Bass (sine waves + 808s)
├── Lead Synths (heavily processed)
├── Vocal Main (pitched down, reverb)
├── Vocal Texture (granular processed)
├── Samples (occult/horror elements)
├── Noise/Atmosphere (industrial textures)
└── Bus Effects (parallel compression)
```

### Critical Mix Techniques

**1. Frequency Separation**
- High-pass everything except bass/kick at 120Hz minimum
- Dedicated sub-bass channel (mono below 80Hz)
- Aggressive midrange scooping (200Hz-2kHz)

**2. Spatial Processing**
- Reverb on 80%+ of elements (cathedral/hall algorithms)
- Stereo delay throws for rhythmic elements  
- M/S processing for width without muddiness
- Multiple reverb returns (close/medium/far ambiences)

**3. Dynamics & Distortion**
- Parallel compression with extreme ratios (10:1+)
- Multiple stages of subtle saturation
- Sidechain compression from kick to create pump
- Bus compression with character units (VCA, tube, etc.)

### Technical Specifications
- **Target LUFS:** -18 to -14 (louder than ambient, quieter than EDM)
- **Peak Limiting:** -1dBFS maximum, preserve transients
- **Stereo Width:** Wide but stable (avoid phase issues in clubs)
- **Format Considerations:** High-resolution for streaming, maintain low-end translation

## Reasoning Chains

### Compositional Logic
1. **Horror film structure** - Tension building, jump scares, resolution
2. **Occult ritual pacing** - Slow build, climax, aftermath
3. **Dream-state navigation** - Non-linear, emotional rather than logical
4. **Underground accessibility** - Deliberately obscured but rewarding discovery

### Emotional Manipulation
- **Unease through dissonance** - Avoiding resolution, maintaining tension
- **Power through volume contrast** - Whisper-to-scream dynamics
- **Mystery through obscurity** - Hidden elements revealed on repeat listening
- **Catharsis through noise** - Release of tension through sonic assault

### Cultural Positioning
- **Anti-mainstream stance** - Rejection of commercial accessibility
- **Internet-native distribution** - Designed for digital discovery
- **Aesthetic coherence** - Sound married to visual occult imagery
- **Community gatekeeping** - Unicode symbols preventing easy searching

## Canonical Tracks

### Foundation Era (2009-2011)
- **Salem - "Asia"** *(King Night, 2010)* - Genre-defining industrial synths and percussion
- **Clams Casino - "I'm God"** *(2011)* - Ethereal cloud rap template, heavily sampled
- **oOoOO - "Sedsumting"** *(2010)* - Textural hip-hop mesh with ghoulish vocals
- **White Ring - "lxC999"** *(Black Earth That Made Me, 2010)* - Droning synths, unintelligible vocals
- **Balam Acab - "See Birds"** *(2010)* - Natural sound collaging, atmospheric spaces
- **Holy Other - "Know Where"** *(With U, 2010)* - Multi-genre ethereal sound

### Revival Era (2019+)
- **Sematary - "Slaughterhouse"** - Modern horror trap interpretation
- **Wicca Phase Springs Eternal - "Just One Thing"** - Emo-witch house fusion
- **Death Grips - "Hacker"** - Industrial witch house influence
