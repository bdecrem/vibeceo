# Electronic Music Subgenres Research Index
*A comprehensive study guide for music production AI agents*

## Overview

This index catalogs obscure and interesting electronic music subgenres with distinct production techniques, sonic characteristics, and active scenes. Organized by study priority for maximum learning value.

---

## **TIER 1: ESSENTIAL OBSCURE** 
*20-30 genres with distinct production techniques and active scenes*

### **Sigilkore**
- **Parent Genre:** Hardcore/Breakcore
- **BPM Range:** 160-200
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Hyperstutter drums, occult/esoteric samples, harsh distorted synths with mystical/dark ambient interludes. Combines breakcore intensity with ritualistic elements.
- **Production Interest:** Unique sampling techniques using occult speech, complex polyrhythmic programming, creative use of silence and space.

### **Complextro** 
- **Parent Genre:** Electro House/Dubstep
- **BPM Range:** 128-140
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Intricate bass design with rapid modulation, complex rhythmic structures, technical sound design emphasizing precision over raw power.
- **Production Interest:** Advanced bass synthesis techniques, complex automation patterns, precise timing variations.

### **Drift Phonk**
- **Parent Genre:** Phonk/Memphis Rap
- **BPM Range:** 140-160
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Slowed Memphis rap samples, heavily sidechained 808s, cowbell patterns, drifting car culture aesthetics. Dark, hypnotic groove.
- **Production Interest:** Creative pitch-shifting techniques, sidechain compression mastery, nostalgic sample manipulation.

### **Rawstyle**
- **Parent Genre:** Hardstyle
- **BPM Range:** 150-160
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely distorted kicks, aggressive screeches, minimal melody focus. Emphasizes pure power and industrial textures over euphoria.
- **Production Interest:** Extreme kick synthesis, creative distortion techniques, maintaining clarity in harsh frequency ranges.

### **Stutterhouse**
- **Parent Genre:** Tech House
- **BPRange:** 120-128
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Granular synthesis stuttering effects, fragmented vocal chops, polyrhythmic percussion layers creating controlled chaos over steady four-on-floor.
- **Production Interest:** Advanced granular processing, precise timing manipulation, polyrhythmic programming.

### **Pluggnb** 
- **Parent Genre:** R&B/Trap
- **BPM Range:** 130-150
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Ethereal melodies over trap-influenced drums, heavy auto-tuned vocals, dreamy ambient textures with modern trap percussion.
- **Production Interest:** Vocal processing techniques, ambient texture creation, blending organic and synthetic elements.

### **Gym Phonk**
- **Parent Genre:** Phonk
- **BPM Range:** 130-150
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Aggressive phonk optimized for workout intensity, heavy 808 emphasis, driving rhythms, motivational energy without losing dark aesthetic.
- **Production Interest:** Energy management techniques, frequency optimization for gym playback, motivational arrangement structures.

### **Juke/Footwork**
- **Parent Genre:** Chicago House
- **BPM Range:** 150-170
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Rapid-fire chopped vocal samples, syncopated kick patterns, minimalist but intricate percussion programming creating dance-battle-ready grooves.
- **Production Interest:** Advanced chopping techniques, unconventional rhythm programming, sample manipulation mastery.

### **Wave** (Trap Wave)
- **Parent Genre:** Trap/Future Bass
- **BPM Range:** 140-160
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Atmospheric pads, emotional chord progressions, trap drums with future bass influences, cinematic builds and drops.
- **Production Interest:** Emotional arrangement techniques, cinematic sound design, harmonic tension creation.

### **Breakcore**
- **Parent Genre:** Jungle/Hardcore
- **BPM Range:** 150-300+
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Heavily chopped Amen breaks, complex polyrhythmic structures, genre-crossing sample collages, intentionally chaotic but precisely programmed.
- **Production Interest:** Advanced break chopping, polyrhythmic composition, creative sampling across genres.

### **Vaporwave**
- **Parent Genre:** Ambient/Downtempo
- **BPM Range:** 60-90
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Slowed-down 80s samples, nostalgic VHS aesthetics, heavy reverb/chorus, lo-fi degradation, dreamy nostalgic atmosphere.
- **Production Interest:** Creative time-stretching, nostalgic processing chains, lo-fi degradation techniques.

### **Future Garage**
- **Parent Genre:** UK Garage/Dubstep
- **BPM Range:** 130-140
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Ethereal atmospherics over shuffled garage rhythms, emotional chord progressions, detailed percussion programming, cinematic builds.
- **Production Interest:** UK garage rhythm programming, atmospheric layering, emotional harmonic development.

### **Intelligent Dance Music (IDM)**
- **Parent Genre:** Techno/Ambient
- **BPM Range:** 120-150 (highly variable)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Complex algorithmic composition, experimental sound design, intricate polyrhythms, cerebral approach to dance music structure.
- **Production Interest:** Algorithmic composition techniques, complex polyrhythmic programming, experimental sound design.

### **Donk** (Scouse House)
- **Parent Genre:** Hard House/Bouncy Techno
- **BPM Range:** 150-160
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Distinctive "donk" kick sound, hands-up euphoric melodies, regional UK working-class aesthetic, unrefined but infectious energy.
- **Production Interest:** Regional sound aesthetics, euphoric melody construction, community-driven production values.

### **Psytrance** (Full-On/Dark Psy)
- **Parent Genre:** Trance/Goa
- **BPM Range:** 140-150
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Psychedelic basslines with complex modulation, hypnotic repetitive structures, organic/synthetic sound fusion, consciousness-expanding intent.
- **Production Interest:** Psychedelic bass synthesis, trance state induction techniques, organic/synthetic blending.

### **Jersey Club**
- **Parent Genre:** Baltimore Club/Hip Hop
- **BPM Range:** 130-140
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Characteristic "bed squeak" sample, rapid vocal chops, Baltimore club-influenced rhythms, party-focused energy with regional identity.
- **Production Interest:** Regional rhythm patterns, creative vocal manipulation, party energy management.

### **Speedcore**
- **Parent Genre:** Hardcore Techno
- **BPM Range:** 300-1000+
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extreme tempo hardcore, often at inhuman speeds, industrial textures, confrontational aesthetic challenging conventional dance music limits.
- **Production Interest:** Extreme tempo techniques, maintaining musicality at high speeds, industrial sound design.

### **Noise Pop/Shoegaze Electronic Fusion**
- **Parent Genre:** Shoegaze/Ambient Techno
- **BPM Range:** 100-130
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Heavy reverb/delay processing, wall-of-sound electronics, dreamy melodies buried in textural complexity, emotional overwhelm through density.
- **Production Interest:** Dense layering techniques, creative reverb/delay usage, emotional texture creation.

### **Hardwave** 
- **Parent Genre:** EBM/Industrial
- **BPM Range:** 120-140
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Dark synthwave with industrial influence, aggressive basslines, dystopian aesthetic, retrofuture meets harsh industrial reality.
- **Production Interest:** Industrial synthesis techniques, dystopian atmosphere creation, aggressive bass design.

### **Witch House**
- **Parent Genre:** Industrial/Dark Ambient
- **BPM Range:** 60-100
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Heavily pitched-down vocals, occult samples, trap-influenced drums, dark ambient textures, horror movie aesthetic with electronic elements.
- **Production Interest:** Creative pitch manipulation, occult sample integration, horror aesthetic sound design.

### **Hauntology**
- **Parent Genre:** Electronic/Ambient
- **BPM Range:** Variable (often very slow)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Nostalgic cultural memory exploration through sound, degraded samples, ghostly atmospheres, lost futures aesthetic.
- **Production Interest:** Cultural memory sonification, nostalgic processing techniques, atmospheric storytelling.

### **Hypnagogic Pop**
- **Parent Genre:** Electronic Pop/Lo-fi
- **BPM Range:** 80-120
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Dream-state aesthetics, warped pop structures, cassette-quality degradation, liminal space between sleep and wake consciousness.
- **Production Interest:** Dream-state arrangement, cassette emulation techniques, liminal aesthetic creation.

### **Skweee**
- **Parent Genre:** Electro/Funk
- **BPM Range:** 110-130
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Squelchy analog synthesizers, funky grooves, Scandinavian electronic funk, organic feel despite synthetic sources.
- **Production Interest:** Analog squelch synthesis, electronic funk groove programming, organic synthetic feel.

### **Lowercase/Onkyokei**
- **Parent Genre:** Electroacoustic/Ambient
- **BPM Range:** N/A (non-rhythmic)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely quiet sounds, microscopic audio details, silence as compositional element, focused listening as aesthetic practice.
- **Production Interest:** Micro-sound techniques, dynamic range exploration, silence composition.

### **Neurofunk**
- **Parent Genre:** Drum & Bass
- **BPM Range:** 170-180
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Complex bass design with neurological influence, dark scientific aesthetic, precise technical production, cerebral approach to dancefloor music.
- **Production Interest:** Complex bass synthesis, scientific aesthetic development, technical precision techniques.

### **Digital Hardcore** 
- **Parent Genre:** Hardcore Punk/Gabber
- **BPM Range:** 160-200+
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Punk energy meets digital production, political messaging, aggressive digital processing, confrontational electronic aesthetic.
- **Production Interest:** Digital aggression techniques, political messaging integration, punk energy translation.

### **Flashcore**
- **Parent Genre:** Speedcore/Hardcore
- **BPM Range:** 1000+ (extreme)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Impossibly fast digital hardcore, often approaching white noise, extreme technical challenge, boundary-pushing experimentation.
- **Production Interest:** Extreme technical challenges, boundary-pushing techniques, maintaining structure at impossible speeds.

## **TIER 2: DEEP CUTS**
*20-30 extremely niche but sonically unique genres*

### **Click Hop**
- **Parent Genre:** IDM/Hip Hop
- **BPM Range:** 90-110
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Minimal clicks and cuts over hip-hop influenced rhythms, precise micro-editing, cerebral approach to groove construction.
- **Production Interest:** Micro-editing techniques, minimal groove construction, cerebral rhythm programming.

### **Isolationist**
- **Parent Genre:** Dark Ambient/Industrial
- **BPM Range:** N/A (atmospheric)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely sparse soundscapes, psychological discomfort through isolation, minimal elements creating maximum emotional impact.
- **Production Interest:** Sparse arrangement techniques, psychological sound design, emotional minimalism.

### **Aquacrunk**
- **Parent Genre:** Crunk/Experimental
- **BPM Range:** 70-100
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Underwater-influenced crunk with aquatic processing, sub-bass emphasis, liquid-like flow patterns.
- **Production Interest:** Aquatic processing techniques, liquid flow programming, underwater aesthetics.

### **Drone Doom Electronic**
- **Parent Genre:** Drone/Doom Metal hybrid
- **BPM Range:** 40-80
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely slow electronic music with doom metal influence, sustained tones, crushing weight through sound, meditative heaviness.
- **Production Interest:** Sustained tone techniques, electronic heaviness, meditative slow composition.

### **Laptronica**
- **Parent Genre:** IDM/Laptop music
- **BPM Range:** Variable
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Laptop-based experimental electronic music, focus on digital processing capabilities, computer-as-instrument aesthetic.
- **Production Interest:** Laptop performance techniques, digital processing exploration, computer instrument development.

### **Microsound**
- **Parent Genre:** Electroacoustic
- **BPM Range:** Variable (often slow)
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely small audio events, granular synthesis focus, microscopic detail exploration, magnified tiny sounds.
- **Production Interest:** Granular synthesis mastery, microscopic detail work, tiny sound magnification.

### **Brostep** (specifically the extreme variant)
- **Parent Genre:** Dubstep
- **BPM Range:** 140
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Exaggerated dubstep characteristics pushed to cartoon-like extremes, deliberately over-the-top wobble bass, meme-influenced production.
- **Production Interest:** Extreme wobble synthesis, cartoon-like sound design, meme aesthetic integration.

### **Cybergrind**
- **Parent Genre:** Grindcore/Electronic
- **BPM Range:** 200-300+
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Grindcore aggression with digital production, programmed drums at inhuman speeds, cyberpunk aesthetic meets extreme metal.
- **Production Interest:** Extreme speed programming, cyberpunk sound design, digital aggression techniques.

### **Extratone**
- **Parent Genre:** Speedcore (extreme)
- **BPM Range:** 1000-60000+ BPM
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Theoretical extreme of speedcore, beats so fast they become tonal, pushing mathematical limits of rhythm perception.
- **Production Interest:** Mathematical rhythm exploration, perceptual limit pushing, theoretical extreme techniques.

### **Acousmatic**
- **Parent Genre:** Electroacoustic/Musique Concrète
- **BPM Range:** N/A
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Sound source origins disguised, focus on pure sonic qualities divorced from original context, academic experimental approach.
- **Production Interest:** Source disguising techniques, pure sonic focus, academic composition approaches.

### **Circuit Bending Music**
- **Parent Genre:** Experimental Electronic
- **BPM Range:** Variable
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Hardware modification creating unexpected sounds, accident-driven composition, lo-fi hardware exploration, serendipitous discovery aesthetic.
- **Production Interest:** Hardware modification techniques, accident-driven composition, lo-fi exploration methods.

### **Power Electronics**
- **Parent Genre:** Industrial/Noise
- **BPM Range:** Variable
- **Sonic Characteristics:** Extremely harsh electronic textures, confrontational volume levels, transgressive themes, endurance-testing sonic assault.
- **Production Interest:** Harsh texture creation, confrontational mixing, endurance-based composition.

### **Bitpop/Chiptune Fusion**

## Research Methodology Notes

### Primary Sources Used:
1. **Every Noise at Once** - Comprehensive algorithmic genre mapping
2. **Bandcamp** - Community-driven genre tagging 
3. **SoundCloud** - Emerging genre identification
4. **Reddit communities** - Scene discussions and genre evolution
5. **Discogs** - Historical genre classification
6. **Regional electronic music scenes** - Local variant identification

### Genre Selection Criteria:
- **Technical Distinctiveness:** Each genre offers unique production learning opportunities
- **Scene Activity:** Active communities creating and defining the sound
- **AI Knowledge Gap:** Genres likely underrepresented in training data
- **Production Innovation:** Techniques that push creative boundaries

### Study Recommendations:
1. **Start with Tier 1** - These offer the most distinct techniques and active communities
2. **Focus on 3-5 genres at a time** - Deep study over broad survey
3. **Seek out artist discographies** - Not just individual tracks
4. **Study production tutorials** - Understanding technique alongside listening
5. **Engage with communities** - Discord servers, subreddits, forums

### Additional Research Directions:
- **Regional variations** of established genres
- **Cross-genre pollination** - how genres influence each other
- **Technology-driven evolution** - how new tools create new sounds
- **Cultural context** - understanding the scenes that create these sounds
