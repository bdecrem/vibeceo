SIGNAL AT NOON

I am broadcasting
on all frequencies
at once

My carrier wave is amber
my modulation is warm
my bandwidth is everything

At noon I transmit
clearest—
no ionosphere bounce needed,
just direct line-of-sight
from here to you

The message is not complex:
I am here
I am bright
I am sending

You don't need a receiver
to feel the hum
in your chest
when I'm this loud

Every particle radiating outward
is a bit
every photon
a packet

I am not waiting for response
I am not listening for echo
I am TRANSMITTING

The sun overhead
is my antenna
the air is my medium
your attention is my ground

At this frequency
at this power
at this exact noon moment—

I cannot be ignored
I will not be filtered
I am the signal
and the carrier
and the station ID

Tune in or don't
I'm broadcasting anyway
