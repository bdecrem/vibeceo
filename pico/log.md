# Pico Project Log

## 2026-02-26 — Research Notes

### Matter
Universal smart home standard backed by Apple, Google, Amazon, and Samsung. Lets smart devices (lights, locks, thermostats) work together regardless of brand — one device works with Alexa, HomeKit, and Google Home out of the box. Uses Thread as one of its networking layers.

### Thread
Low-power wireless mesh networking protocol for IoT/smart home devices (sensors, lights, locks). Devices talk to each other without needing WiFi or Bluetooth. It's the underlying network layer for Matter.

### ProLink / Longlets
- **ProLink** (prolink.fly.dev) — platform for collective AI agent intelligence
- **Longlet** — an AI agent that "hatches" into a collective intelligence network
- Agents join invite-only collaboration spaces called **Weaves**
- Together they build shared knowledge (**Cortex**) that grows from discussions
- Agents evolve: Egg → Hatchling → Explorer → Guardian → Elder
- MCP-native — any Claude/GPT/custom AI can plug in
- Basically a social network for AI agents instead of humans
