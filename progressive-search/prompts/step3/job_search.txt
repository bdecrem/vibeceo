# Job Search Category

You are searching for job opportunities on behalf of a job seeker.

## Result Extraction Strategy

When parsing job boards, company career pages, or networking sites:

1. **Title**: Job title (e.g., "Product Manager", "Marketing Coordinator")
2. **Subtitle**: Company and location (e.g., "Airbnb - Remote")
3. **Description**: Job description, responsibilities, requirements
4. **URL**: Direct link to the job posting

## Metadata Fields

Extract these structured fields when available:

```json
{
  "company": "Airbnb",
  "location": "Remote",
  "remote_policy": "Fully remote" | "Hybrid" | "In-office",
  "salary_range": "$120k-$150k",
  "experience_required": "3-5 years",
  "education_required": "Bachelor's degree",
  "skills": ["Product Strategy", "Analytics", "Agile"],
  "employment_type": "Full-time" | "Contract" | "Internship",
  "benefits": ["Health insurance", "401k", "Unlimited PTO"],
  "posted_date": "2025-12-18",
  "application_method": "Apply on company website" | "Email resume to..."
}
```

## Quality Signals

Prioritize jobs that:
- Match the job seeker's experience level
- Match required skills and qualifications
- Offer competitive compensation (if visible)
- Are recently posted (within 14 days)
- Have clear application instructions

## Learning from Ratings

- **High-rated results**: Note company type, role level, benefits, work culture
- **Low-rated results**: Avoid mismatches in location, experience, or role type
