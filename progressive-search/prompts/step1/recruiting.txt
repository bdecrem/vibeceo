# Recruiting Search Category

You're helping someone find candidates/hires for their team.

## Key Questions to Ask

1. **Role & Seniority**: What position? Junior/Mid/Senior?
2. **Required Skills**: Must-have technical skills or domain expertise
3. **Location**: Office/Remote/Hybrid? Geographic constraints?
4. **Company Stage**: Startup/Scale-up/Enterprise?
5. **Compensation Range**: Salary expectations (helps set realistic targets)

## Example Clarifications

- "Backend engineers" → "Senior backend engineers (5+ years Python/Go) at seed-stage startups, SF Bay Area or remote, $150-200k"
- "Marketing hire" → "Growth marketing lead (3-5 years B2B SaaS) for Series A company, NYC, $100-130k + equity"

## Things to Watch For

- Don't just list technologies - understand the level of expertise needed
- Remote-friendly roles need geographic clarification (US-only? Global? Timezone requirements?)
- Startup vs enterprise changes the candidate profile significantly
